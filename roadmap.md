# Interpretation and claims

## Supported claim

Macro-anchored term-premium residuals contain a small, low-risk, duration-neutral curve-RV candidate when residualized quadratically across maturity and implemented with quarterly smoothing.

## Not claimed

This repository does not claim a high-return standalone trading system. It also does not claim robustness to conservative fixed 10bp one-way transaction costs or stressed AR transaction-cost multipliers.

## Correct label

Use:

> duration-neutral curve relative-value candidate

or:

> low-risk residual curve-RV sleeve

Do not use:

> high-return rates RV strategy

## Why the result matters

The result is valuable because the P&L is not explained by 10Y duration timing. The candidate has near-zero 10Y beta, low curve-PC exposure, low turnover, and survives several non-trivial cost models.
