# Transaction costs

V6 evaluates the RV candidates under several cost assumptions:

- Corwin-Schultz lagged ETF proxy costs;
- Abdi-Ranaldo-style lagged ETF proxy costs;
- fixed 2.5bp, 5bp, 7.5bp, 10bp, 15bp, and 25bp one-way costs;
- stressed AR multipliers.

The headline projection strategy survives CS, AR and fixed 5bp costs, but fails fixed 10bp and stressed AR assumptions. This is why the repository is framed as a cautious curve-RV candidate rather than an extremely robust production strategy.

Cost estimates are proxies, not executable quotes.
